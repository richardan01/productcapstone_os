## Quick Summary

**Problem**: CrossFit coaches can't personalize programming beyond ~15 athletes. Athletes plateau on generic programs.

**Solution**: AI coaching co-pilot that watches athlete videos, detects movement issues, and drafts coach-ready feedback — without replacing the coach.

**One-sentence UVP**:
> "An always-on CrossFit session intelligence agent that turns training videos into prioritized coaching insights and ready-to-send athlete feedback."

**Core Personas**:
- **Coach Jason** — CrossFit coach at Tanjong Pagar, manages 40 athletes, can only personally program for 12
- **Wei Lin** — 31yo Investment Banking Associate, trains 5x/week, wants competition prep, can't afford S$500/mo coaching

**Business Model**: B2B2C SaaS
- B2B (coaches): S$99/mo base + S$5/athlete/mo
- B2C (athletes): S$39–59/mo (Phase 2)

**Target Market**: Singapore CrossFit (30+ affiliates, 5,000–8,000 active athletes) → SEA expansion

**Build deadline**: April 15, 2026
